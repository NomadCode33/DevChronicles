About Me

I am endlessly curious!

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve always wondered how the world works. No matter how many times I tried to blend in, I always stood out—and deep down, I knew there was something more beneath the surface. I asked a lot of questions because I felt something deeper was waiting to be discovered.

My love for creativity and ingenuity found its first outlet through a computer. I was obsessed. There were limitless ways to do things, countless worlds to explore, and endless things to learn. I still remember experimenting on my dad’s computer—going on Cartoon Network (because I loved cartoons) and playing games. That curiosity led me somewhere I didn’t expect: into the world of coding—and later, to a deep passion for GIS.

Coding opened a new world for me. It gave me the ability to create, to build something real and useful from nothing but an idea. I saw the potential to shape lives, improve systems, and make a difference—one line of code at a time.

GIS, on the other hand, introduced me to the soul of places. It’s more than just mapmaking—it's about connection. It's about understanding the land, its people, and the stories that shaped them. What makes the community unique? Why are people so connected to their culture and history? Those questions matter. A well-informed map isn’t just a visual—it’s a bridge to deeper understanding, and it deserves to represent the truth of the land and the lives on it.

This drive to question everything has only grown with time. I look at the world and often wonder: Why do we accept outdated systems when everything around us is changing? Throughout history, innovators like Tesla and Einstein pushed against the ordinary. They refused to accept “what is” and instead envisioned what could be. They reshaped the world not just with ideas, but with the courage to act on them.

I hold that same spirit within me. I don’t fit into molds—I break them. I’m always exploring new ways to create, to uplift, and to challenge the status quo with curiosity, creativity, and heart.

I am endlessly curious, relentlessly driven, and deeply committed to uncovering the why behind everything.

I’m here to build a better future—code by code, map by map.
